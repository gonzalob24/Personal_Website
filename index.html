    About Me

    I am currently a senior at the University of Houston – Clear Lake, majoring in computer science and will graduate this December. This year, I focused on participating in internships and designing projects using various front and back end tools. I recently completed an internship at NASA where I developed 2 Python modules that were used for machine learning. For my senior project, I am creating a sports data API using machine learning for NBA game predictions. My proposal was one of the four out of the 30 proposals to be selected.

    My passion is designing web development or machine learning applications. They may seem like two completely different fields, but I find them to be very similar. They both allow me to play with different ideas: through trial and error, I develop a product I am ecstatic to share.
